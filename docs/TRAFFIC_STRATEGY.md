# Traffic Strategy

## SEO

Focus on indexable pages with specific intent: local-time schedule, today matches, standings, leaderboards, and team schedules. Expect slow ramp-up before the tournament and stronger growth near matchdays.

## Social

Use shareable cards for repeatable formats. Keep copy short and link to relevant pages. Do not expect every post to drive traffic; the goal is recognition and repeat clicks.

## Telegram

Telegram can work for daily match reminders. Start only if there is capacity to post consistently.

## Reddit

Avoid spam. Share genuinely useful local-time schedule links only in relevant discussions. Participate before posting links.

## Direct Traffic

Make the site easy to remember and fast. Returning users will come from schedule utility, not marketing copy.

## Partnerships

Small football pages, newsletters, and fan communities may share cards or schedule pages. Offer useful assets first, not pitches.

## Realistic Expectations

Early traffic may be low until Google indexes pages and tournament interest rises. Treat Search Console impressions, not visits, as the first signal.
